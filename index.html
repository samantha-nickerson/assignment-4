Bring Your Swimsuit!

This beautiful new facility houses the Kinsmen Pool, a six-lane 25 meter tank, along with a two-storey waterslide, toddler leisure pool and spacious whirlpool.

Bring your swimsuit, towel and sense of adventure! Aquafitness; water sports (basketball, volleyball, underwater hockey, water polo); swimming for adults, early birds, preschool, rehab and leisure; lifesaving and lifeguard courses; Red Cross Learn to Swim and First Aid are just some of the programs offered.

We welcome schools for physical education classes, special occasions and end of the year celebrations. Need a fundraiser? How about a "tread-a-athon" or "slide-a-thon"? And don't forget to bring your friends for a birthday party! Our possibilities are limited only by your imagination!

Swimming is just the beginning... Your new Aquatic Centre is the place to build friendships, confidence, skills and well-being - a healthier community for all.

We hope to see you at the pool!
